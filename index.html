About

My name is Kevin Nel. I am a musician and producer that currently makes instrumental music under the artist name "Half Deep" I am a sucker for a good ambient piece and appreciate musics ability to convey emotion and feelings in a unique way compared to other mediums.


[Ambient] Music must be able to accommodate many levels of listening attention without enforcing one in particular; it must be as ignorable as it is interesting. ~ Brian Eno
