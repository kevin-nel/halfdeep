About

If you didnt already gather, my name is Half Deep. I am a musician and producer that currently makes instrumental music. I am a sucker for a good ambient piece and love music that that conveys emotions. One of my favorite music quotes is by the great Brian Eno


[Ambient] Music must be able to accommodate many levels of listening attention without enforcing one in particular; it must be as ignorable as it is interesting. ~ Brian Eno
